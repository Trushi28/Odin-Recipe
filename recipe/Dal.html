Dal

Description

Dal is a comforting and nutritious lentil dish seasoned with Indian spices, making it a staple in Indian households.

Ingredients

  • Toor dal (200 g)
  • Onions and tomatoes (1 each, finely chopped)
  • Garlic cloves (2-3, minced)
  • Spices (turmeric, cumin seeds, red chili powder)
  • Ghee or oil
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh coriander leaves

Steps to Prepare

  1. Wash the dal and cook it in a pressure cooker with water, turmeric, and salt until soft.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add garlic, onions, and tomatoes. Cook until soft.
  4. Mix the cooked dal with the tempering and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or chapati.
