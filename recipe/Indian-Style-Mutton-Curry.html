Indian Style Mutton Curry

Description

Mutton Curry is a rich, spicy dish made with tender mutton pieces and a flavorful blend of Indian spices. It pairs perfectly with steamed rice or naan.

Ingredients

  • Mutton (1 kg, cleaned)
  • Onions (3 large, finely chopped)
  • Tomatoes (3 large, pureed)
  • Garlic paste (1 tablespoon)
  • Ginger paste (1 tablespoon)
  • Yogurt (200 g)
  • Spices (coriander, cumin, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Cooking oil or ghee

Steps to Prepare

  1. Marinate the mutton with yogurt, salt, and spices. Let it rest for 1-2 hours.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  3. Add garlic and ginger paste, followed by the tomato puree. Cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add the marinated mutton and cook on medium heat until the spices blend well.
  5. Add water and cook the curry until the mutton is tender.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.
