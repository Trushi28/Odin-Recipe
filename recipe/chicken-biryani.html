Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic dish made with spiced chicken, rice, and a blend of herbs. It's a beloved meal across South Asia and beyond.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (1 kg, cut into pieces)
  • Basmati rice (500 g)
  • Yogurt (200 g)
  • Onions (3 large, thinly sliced)
  • Tomatoes (2 large, chopped)
  • Garlic paste (1 tablespoon)
  • Ginger paste (1 tablespoon)
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala)
  • Bay leaves (2)
  • Green chilies (4-5, slit)
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves (for garnish)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Cooking oil or ghee

Steps to Prepare

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, spices, garlic, and ginger paste. Let it sit for an hour.
  3. Fry the onions until golden brown and set aside.
  4. Cook the marinated chicken with tomatoes, green chilies, and fried onions.
  5. Boil the rice with bay leaves, salt, and a few whole spices until it's half-cooked. Drain the water.
  6. Layer the chicken and rice in a pot. Garnish with coriander, mint leaves, and fried onions.
  7. Seal the pot and cook on low heat (dum) for 25-30 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with raita or salad.

Fun Fact

Did you know? Biryani is believed to have originated from Persia and was brought to the Indian subcontinent by the Mughals!
