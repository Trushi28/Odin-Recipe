Paneer Tikka

Description

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer made by marinating paneer (cottage cheese) cubes in spiced yogurt and grilling them to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (300 g, cut into cubes)
  • Yogurt (200 g)
  • Bell peppers and onions (diced)
  • Spices (turmeric, red chili powder, cumin powder, garam masala)
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Skewers

Steps to Prepare

  1. Mix yogurt with spices, salt, and lemon juice to create the marinade.
  2. Add paneer cubes, bell peppers, and onions to the marinade. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Thread the marinated ingredients onto skewers.
  4. Grill or bake until the paneer is slightly charred.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney.
